field_eyebrow: Who We Are

A Century of Scientific Innovation,
A Future of Global Impact

Founded in 1924 as a small laboratory in New York, AxisPharma has grown into one of the world's leading pharmaceutical corporations — driven by the belief that science, executed with integrity and scaled with discipline, can transform patient lives globally.

Innovation Integrity Impact
  • Founded 1924, Headquartered New York, NY
  • 18 products on WHO Essential Medicines List
  • 12 WHO-GMP & FDA-cleared manufacturing facilities

  • 50,000 employees across R&D, manufacturing, commercial, & corporate functions
